🧍 Who I Am

I’ve always wanted to make games — and one day I just decided to go for it. Now I’m studying game development at university and diving into everything from programming to design, learning the tools and figuring it out as I go.

I’m into competitive shooters, movement-based games, roguelites, and anything with a solid story. I like games that feel sharp, fast, or meaningful — ideally all three. I’m usually pretty quiet and reserved, but with the right people, I open up. I love experimenting and seeing what sticks.

Right now, I’m focused on learning and building. Long term, I see myself in indie dev — unless a studio actually earns my respect and gives me something real to be a part of.
